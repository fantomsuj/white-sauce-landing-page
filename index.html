Where Flavor Takes Center Stage

Since opening our doors on Franklin Street in May 2025, White Sauce Grill & Bar has become Downtown Chapel Hill's go-to destination for Mediterranean gyros and platters. Our culinary artisans craft extraordinary dishes using the finest all-natural ingredients.

Our signature falafel, gyro, and chicken over rice are lovingly prepared to perfection, complemented by a tantalizing array of housemade sauces. Each bite promises a harmonious blend of flavors that will leave you craving more.
